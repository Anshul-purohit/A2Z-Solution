Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a national level examination which is used to take admissions in TOP IITs/NITs for higher education or in PSUs (Public Sector Undertakings).
In this article, I'm mainly focusing on CSE branch on how to crack GATE CSE.

  • My first advise would be to start your preparation with Discrete Mathematics, because it somehow relates to every CSE subjects.
  • If you are in college then you can prepare the subject which is currently in your semester, that way it will help you in college exams also.
  • There are some subjects which required some prerequisite, do them in following order:
    • Digital logic before Computer Architecture and Computer Networks.
    • Theory of Computation before Compiler Design.
  • After completing every subject, try to solve its previous year questions and analyze them properly.
  • Try to take atleast one Test Series to track your progress.
  • One thing to remember is, it's a very long journey so start with the subjects which you are confident in and gradually move to difficult subjects.
  • There will be time when you feel like giving up, just remember the reason why you started and don't quit.
    "When the going gets tough, the tough get going."
